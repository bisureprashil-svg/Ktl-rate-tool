Drive times are truck estimates: straight-line distance between each region's mid-point × road factor (1.45), at a city truck speed (~35 km/h), plus 10 min per leg for a loaded container truck — all set in Manage Rates → Settings. They approximate Google's road time but aren't live. For an exact lane, type the real minutes in any leg and hit 💾 Save these times — that route then auto-fills (shown as saved) on every future trip. Highway lanes (e.g. Deltaport, Abbotsford) read high until saved, since distance alone can't tell highway from city.
